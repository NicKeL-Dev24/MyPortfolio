About Me

I'm a results-driven frontend developer specializing in building modern, scalable web applications that deliver exceptional user experiences. With deep expertise in React and Vue.js ecosystems, I architect component-based solutions that are maintainable, performant, and visually stunning.

My technical arsenal includes the latest ES6+ JavaScript features, modern CSS frameworks like Tailwind and Bootstrap, and a mobile-first approach to responsive design. I excel at transforming complex requirements into intuitive interfaces, writing clean and semantic HTML5, crafting smooth CSS3 animations, and implementing interactive functionality that enhances user engagement.

Whether it's rapid prototyping with utility-first frameworks, building reusable component libraries, or optimizing performance for production environments, I bring a perfect blend of technical proficiency and creative problem-solving to every project. I'm passionate about staying current with industry trends, writing maintainable code, and collaborating with teams to turn ambitious visions into reality. Let's build something remarkable together.

Intramurals Leaderboard and Points System

Designed to systematically track and rank college intramural teams, the Intramurals Leaderboard and Point System records points for performance, participation, and sportsmanship to ensure transparency, fairness, and organized competition.

Cozy Paradise: Hotel Booking System

Cozy Paradise is a user-friendly hotel booking platform designed to simplify room reservations while providing a cozy and seamless booking experience.

Pay to Park System

Designed for students, staff, and visitors with vehicles near USeP, the Pay to Park System streamlines parking by providing real-time slot availability, fast digital payments, and a hassle-free entry and exit experience—making campus parking simple, convenient, and stress-free.

LibraTrack System

Designed to organize and manage important library documents at USeP Library, LibraTrack System streamlines cataloging, tracking, and retrieval, making library management efficient and hassle-free for staff and users.

EchoMach

EchoMach is a rental management system for Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering that provides students access to tractors and essential machinery equipment for academic use.

U'Sign

KoiDex

KoiDex is a mobile app designed for Pokémon fans to rent and manage a Pokédex. It lets players track, organize, and explore Pokémon collections, providing a fun and flexible way to complete their Dex and strategize without needing to catch every Pokémon themselves.
